Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vynix ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the Bhagavad Gītā mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Summary

  • The App does not require an account and does not ask you for your name, email, or phone number.
  • Your reading progress, bookmarks, notes, and preferences are stored only on your device.
  • We show advertisements via Google AdMob and its mediation partners. These ad networks may collect device and usage data to serve and measure ads.
  • For users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, we request consent for ads through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) before personalized ads are shown.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Information stored on your device

The following is kept locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Reading progress, streaks, and the verses you have read.
  • Saved verses (bookmarks) and personal notes you create.
  • App preferences (theme, font size, reading languages, daily-reminder time).
  • Recent searches.

2.2 Information collected by advertising and infrastructure partners

To display ads and operate the App, our third-party partners may collect or receive:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID) and approximate, IP-based location.
  • Device information (model, operating system, language, network state).
  • Ad interaction and performance data (impressions, clicks, crashes).

2.3 Notifications

If you enable daily reading reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server, and no notification data leaves your device.

4. How Information Is Used

  • To provide and maintain the App's reading features.
  • To display, personalize (where consented), and measure advertisements.
  • To remember your preferences and reading progress on your device.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and abuse.

5. Consent & Your Choices (GDPR / ePrivacy)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we present a consent form (via Google UMP) on first launch. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time using the Privacy Options control in the App's settings. If you decline, you will see non-personalized or no ads, as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights (CCPA / Other Regions)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Because the App stores your content locally, you can delete it at any time by removing the relevant notes/bookmarks or by uninstalling the App. For requests involving our advertising partners, please use the device-level controls below or contact us.

  • Reset advertising ID: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Delete advertising ID".

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention & Security

Content you create is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data handled by our advertising and infrastructure partners is retained according to their respective policies. We use reasonable technical measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Transfers

Our third-party partners may process data on servers located outside your country. Where required, these transfers are governed by appropriate safeguards under the partners' privacy frameworks.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
